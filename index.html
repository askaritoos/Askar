about the anime

definition

Blue Lock is a Japanese manga series written by Muneyuki Kaneshiro and illustrated by Yuusuke Nomura. It has been published in Weekly Shōnen Magazine Kodansha since August 2018, and as of October 2022, the chapters have been collected into 21 tankōbon volumes.

plot

According to the plot, after the loss of the Japanese team at the 2018 World Cup, a special Blue Lock project was developed to "create" the best and most selfish striker. To survive in the Blue Prison, you need to "walk over the corpses of the rest", because the departure means the end of the entire football life ... Only 1 out of 300 participants will reach the end! It's a manga about selfish football!
